Figs and Toasted Almonds Brie

Description

Brie baked with figs and toasted almonds, a great appetizer for holidays.

Ingredients (12 servings)

  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 6 fresh figs, stemmed and quartered
  • 1 (14 ounce) round 4 ¼-inch diameter round Brie cheese
  • ½ cup toasted almonds
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
